A BIT ABOUTOUR PAST

I think it is necessary to help wild animals. Many of them are in danger now. For example, pandas which live in the jungle and white bears which live in the Arctic. White bears eat fish. If people pollute rivers and seas, animals die. All woods are home to animals and birds. It is important to take care of them. If trees are damaged and flowers and grass are cut, birds and animals will become instinct. Each of us can do something to help nature. We must not frighten animals and birds. We must never break plants or baby trees. There are a lot of ways to help our planet. We must take care of it.
